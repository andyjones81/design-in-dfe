Principles

Following these principles, along with the design standards, means you’ll be designing good user expereicnes.

In addition to the principles and design standards, interaction designers must ensure they also follow the guidelines in the How to apply the Service Standard in DfE manual.

Download the principles poster


The design team in the Service Delivery Division (SDD) have developed these principles and you can download a poster which can be used in show and tells, presentations and workshops.

Open larger version in new tab (JPG 2.3mb) to save or print the poster.

The 9 principles

  • 1. Understand ALL users; needs, behaviours and pain points

  • 2. Design accessibly

  • 3. Follow Gestalt laws

  • 4. Reduce cognitive load

  • 5. Support and prevent users from making mistakes

  • 6. Make users do less

  • 7. Consider context

  • 8. Design a seamless experience

  • 9. Design with data and design logic
