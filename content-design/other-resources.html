Link up across the profession

You can do this by:

  • joining the DfE content design community Slack channel to contribute to discussions with other DfE content designers
  • joining the cross government Slack channel to contribute to discussions with content designers and other professionals working across government
  • following the GDS blog ; this features stories on how GDS is leading the digital transformation of the UK government
  • following the D&T blog ; this features stories on how Digital and Technology are building digital capabilities and services at DfE
  • using the DfE content community Trello board to find out about the community and what’s coming up at weekly community sessions
  • joining our community every Thursday from 1 to 2pm (sessions are also recorded)
